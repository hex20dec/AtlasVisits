What is Atlas Visits

Atlas Visits was created to offer guests beautiful luxury places to stay in South Florida. Set among the most famous beaches and cityscapes, South Florida is the perfect destination for lovers of culture, adventure, city life, food and everything in between! From rooftop drinks in Downtown Miami to evenings filled with music on Calle Ocho, the warmth and sunshine of Florida cannot be missed.

Atlas Visits Impact

At Atlas Visits, we are passionate about reducing our impact on the environment and making a positive change on the community.

Sustainability

  • Sustainability lies at the core of who we are as a business. We recycle all materials where possible and are constantly exploring ways to improve on this.

  • Atlas Visits plants a tree per guest stay to offset carbon emissions.

  • Toxic free cleaning - we always use toxin free cleaning products instead of harsh cleaning chemicals.

  • We minimize the use of single-use plastics and packaging throughout our properties. We are constantly on the lookout for ways to reduce this further.
